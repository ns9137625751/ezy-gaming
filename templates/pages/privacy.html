Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Ezy Gaming Zone's website or services, we may collect:

  • Account information — name, email address, mobile number when you register or book.
  • Booking data — station type, session duration, booking dates.
  • Contact messages — when you fill our contact form.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, browser type (via server logs).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your gaming session bookings.
  • To send booking confirmation and important updates via email or SMS.
  • To respond to enquiries submitted via the contact form.
  • To manage tournament registrations and notify participants.
  • To improve our website and services based on usage patterns.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data with service providers (e.g. payment processors, email services) only as necessary to operate our services, and only under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in to your account. These cookies are essential for the website to function and do not track you across other sites. We do not use advertising or analytics tracking cookies.

5. Data Retention

We retain booking and account data for up to 2 years for operational and accounting purposes. Contact form messages are kept for 1 year. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text. Session tokens are HMAC-signed and HttpOnly. We use HTTPS for all data in transit. While we take reasonable precautions, no internet service is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@ezygaming.in.
